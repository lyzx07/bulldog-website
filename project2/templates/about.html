About Northwest American Bulldogs - Portland, Oregon

    We are a family in Hillsboro, Oregon that has been raising purebred NKC registered American Bulldogs for past 10 years. Our passion for these dogs became our preferred past time and our focus has been to raise healthy, strong, confident, and social puppies that make perfect members of the family. We have had several litters throughout the years, and we keep in contact with most of the families who have purchased a puppy through us. We love receiving photos, videos, and updates on all our puppies.

    We commit to providing healthy puppies to our clients and so we have had our dogs genetically health tested to help ensure healthy litters. We always get health exams for the puppies, deworm them, and vaccinate them before they go home to their forever families. Even with these precautions, if an unforeseen health issue comes up within the first year, we will take any puppy back in exchange for another pup. We feed our dogs a diet of ground raw meat (beef, lamb, chicken), carrots, apples, and other veggies. We do introduce our puppies to soggy Pedigree dog food at 4 weeks and then some dry food by 6 weeks so that they will be used to a dog food diet if that is what you choose to do. They will also receive the raw food diet.
